Welcome To COHAAF

You're The Hope Of Others

CHIZARAM OGAZI HOPE AND AID FOUNDATION (COHAAF) is a beacon of light and hope to the needy- particularly the children widows and those who are affected by social discriminations.

Our Mission

Improving the lives of lots especially the needy in our midst, by providing resources and equipping them with necessary skills and empowerment in their everyday living.

Our Vision

Taking care of the needy, orphans and widows within the community.

We've been saving sight and changing lives around the world.

Just as commanded in the Holy Book - Mark 12: 31 by Almighty God to "Love your neighbor"

The organization is built on the principle of identifying and reaching out to those in need with care and material support.
